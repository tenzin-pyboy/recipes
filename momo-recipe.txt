Ingredients:
  - pieces of 1 kg beef
  - 2 onion
  - 1 garlic
  - 3 spoon of table salt
  - 2 spoon of soya vinager
  - 3 spoon of oil
  - 2 kg of dought 

Direction :
  1. Mix well all beef, onion, garlic, salt, vinager and oil.   
  2. Make small circle of dought.
  3. Add a spoon of mixed beef, onion, ...etc on each circle of dought.
  4. Close them well.
  5. steam cook them for 20 minutes.
